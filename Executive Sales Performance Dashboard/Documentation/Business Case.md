
# Business Case

## Project Title

Executive Sales Performance Dashboard

---

## Background

Business leaders require timely and accurate sales information to make informed decisions. Without a centralized reporting solution, it becomes difficult to monitor revenue performance, product sales, customer behavior, and regional trends.

This project was developed to transform raw sales data into an interactive dashboard that supports business decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Management needs answers to questions such as:

- How much revenue has the business generated?
- Which products contribute the most revenue?
- Which customers are the most valuable?
- Which states generate the highest sales?
- Is revenue increasing or decreasing over time?
- Are customers returning to make additional purchases?

Manually answering these questions is time-consuming and can delay important business decisions.

---

## Project Objectives

The dashboard was designed to:

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track monthly revenue and profit trends.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Measure customer retention and churn.
- Compare sales performance across states.
- Provide executives with clear, actionable insights.

---

## Solution

An interactive three-page Power BI dashboard was developed featuring:

- Executive Sales Performance Dashboard
- Product Performance Dashboard
- Customer Analysis Dashboard

The report includes dynamic KPIs, interactive slicers, DAX measures, navigation buttons, and automatically generated business insights.

---

## Expected Business Value

This dashboard helps decision-makers:

- Monitor key business metrics in real time.
- Identify high-performing products and customers.
- Detect sales trends quickly.
- Improve customer retention strategies.
- Support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Units Sold
- Average Order Value
- Repeat Purchase Rate
- Customer Churn Rate

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel (Dataset)
