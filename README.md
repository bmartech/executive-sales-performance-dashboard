# executive-sales-performance-dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard providing executive insights into sales, products, and customer performance.
## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard was developed to provide executives with a comprehensive view of business performance. It enables stakeholders to monitor revenue, profit, customer behaviour, product performance, and regional sales through interactive visualizations and dynamic KPIs.

## Business Problem

Business leaders require quick access to reliable sales information to make informed decisions.

Without a centralized reporting solution, it becomes difficult to identify trends, evaluate product performance, monitor customer behaviour, and compare regional sales.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing an interactive and easy-to-understand reporting solution.

## Key Business Insights

- Revenue performance is tracked monthly.

- Snickerdoodle generated the highest revenue.

- Park & Shop Convenience Stores is the highest revenue customer.

- Customer retention remained high.

- Alabama generated the highest sales.

- Interactive filters allow users to explore the data by product, customer, state, and date.
